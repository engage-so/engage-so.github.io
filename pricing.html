Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No, you do not.

Can I try things out before paying?

Yes. We have a free account for up to 1,000 active users.

What is "active user" and how do you calculate it?

We define an active user as a customer or account that an event has been triggered for or has recieved a message (email, SMS or other) during the billing period.

What payment methods do you support?

We support major debit and credit cards through Stripe, a leader in online payments.

What happens if I go over my active user limit?

We will charge you at the next billing cycle for the extra active users. We charge $0.01 per active user over the limit.

Do you issue refunds?

If for any reason you want a refund, please reach out to us hello@engage.so within 7 days of payment and we will issue a full refund.

The limits on the plans are too small for us

We can offer a custom plan based on your volume. Please reach out to us: hello@engage.so
