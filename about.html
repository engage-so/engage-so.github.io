Our story

Engage was founded in late 2020 by Opeyemi Obembe. Opeyemi has always been passionate about customer messaging and has built a couple of messaging and telephony solutions.

From his experience building messaging solutions and working with businesses, he discovered sending the right message to customers at the right time through the right channel remains a big challenge for businesses. He experienced this first-hand leading technology for a health maintenance organisation and customer messaging needed to be:

Timely

Delivered when the customer needs it, through the right channel.

Personal

Based on who the customer is and what they have done.
